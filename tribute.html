Edith Södergran

Finnish-Swedish poet and modernist

Short Biography

  • 1892 - Born in St. Petersburg, Russian Empire
  • 1902 - Started her education in Saint Peter's School
  • 1909 - Got diagnosed with tuberculosis and admitted to a sanatorium in Nummela, Finland
  • 1911 - Travelled to Switzerland to seek help for her illness
  • 1916 - Published her debut book, Dikter
  • 1918 - Provoked the first debate about modernism in Swedish by writing a letter to an editor of Dagens Press
  • 1923 - Died at age 31 in her home in Raivola, Finland
